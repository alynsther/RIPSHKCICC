MUMBAI --Power Finance Corp.'s up to 46.6 billion rupees ($1.03 billion) follow-on offering was fully subscribed on the third day. It's the largest public share sale so far this year. 

The state-run power sector lender's 229.55 million shares on offer was bid for 1.49 times by 0730 GMT, according to the latest data on the National Stock Exchange Thursday. The offering closes Friday. 

Power Finance wanted to sell the shares within a range of 193 rupees to 203 rupees a share. At 0810 GMT, the stock was trading at 215.90 rupees on the Bombay Stock Exchange.
