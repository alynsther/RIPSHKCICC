L'Oréal's sales in Brazil rose 15% to €521 million in 2009 (about $702 million at today's rate of exchange). A Friday Marketplace article incorrectly said that L'Oréal's world-wide sales rose 15%.

Duke Energy has agreed to buy Progress Energy in a $13.7 billion deal. A Friday Money & Investing article incorrectly said Progress planned to buy Duke Energy. 

Baltimore Ravens quarterback Trent Dilfer and Tampa Bay Buccaneers quarterback Brad Johnson led their teams to Super Bowl victories in 2000 and 2002, respectively. A Sports article Wednesday incorrectly said the teams won the Super Bowl in consecutive seasons. 

The name of the lead singer of the band Steely Dan is Donald Fagen. His name was misspelled in an Arts & Entertainment article Thursday about the band.
