 Catherine Deneuve starred in the movie "Belle de Jour." A review of her movie "Potiche" in the Friday Journal section misspelled the title "Belle du Jour." 

The authors Harlan Coben and David Foster Wallace attended Amherst College in classes a year apart. An article on the author  Mary Higgins Clark in the Friday Journal section incorrectly said that Messrs. Coben and Wallace were classmates at Dartmouth College. 

 Julian Schnabel's film"Miral" made its premiere at the 2010 Venice Film Festival, five months after producer Harvey Weinstein saw it for the first time. An Arts article on Thursday incorrectly stated that Mr. Weinstein first saw the film after it had screened at the Cannes and Abu Dhabi film festivals.
