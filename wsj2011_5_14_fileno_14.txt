 The Mississippi River is expected to crest at 47.5 feet in Baton Rouge, La., on May 22, above the record of 47.28 feet set in 1927, if the Morganza Floodway isn't opened. A U.S. News article Thursday incorrectly said the forecast incorporated the opening of the Morganza and misstated the 1927 record.
