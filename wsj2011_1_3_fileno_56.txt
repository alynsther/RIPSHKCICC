Florida's Tomas Vokoun made 32 saves as the visiting Rangers suffered their second loss in two days. 

David Booth, Chris Higgins and Stephen Weiss scored for the home team. Martin Biron stopped 32 shots for New York, which is now 22-15-3 on the season. The Rangers, coming off a 2-1 overtime loss in Tampa Bay Saturday night, lost for the first time this season in the second half of back-to-back games. The Rangers' Ruslan Fedotenko left the game in the first period and did not return after blocking a shot with his hand.
