The defending Stanley Cup champion Chicago Blackhawks dismantled the Islanders handily on Sunday, scoring once in the first period and tacking on four more goals in the second to seal the win.

Five different Blackhawks scored against the Isles, who fell to 2-2 this month and 13-21-6 overall. Rookie goaltender Nathan Lawson stopped 40 of 45 shots in the loss.
