HOV Services Ltd. said on Monday that it had entered into a pact to merge its unit HOV Services LLC. with U.S-based Sourcecorp Inc.

Sourcecorp, a Texas-based provider of BPO and specialty consulting services, is a portfolio company of Apollo Management V, L.P., the company said in a regulatory filing.

It didn't give financial details of the deal.

The company said following the merger, HOV Services LLC and Sourcecorp will each control 50% of the combined entity, SCH Services Inc.

The deal is expected to be completed in the next 45-60 days, subject to regulatory approvals, it added.

HOV Services Ltd. said upon closing this deal, the American Depository Receipt offering announced earlier would be cancelled.
