The Jets under Rex Ryan have become a repository for veteran castoffs and players with controversial pasts, and defensive end Trevor Pryce said that philosophy of roster-building figures to continue this offseason. "There are probably 12-15 guys, I know for a fact, he would love to have who are around the league," said Mr. Pryce (pictured above). "When they get released, which they will, they'll be here one way or another."
