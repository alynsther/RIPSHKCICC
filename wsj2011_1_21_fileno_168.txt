The New Jersey Nets snapped a six-game losing streak with a victory over the Utah Jazz on Wednesday night in Newark.

Brook Lopez scored 11 of his 20 points in a big third quarter, propelling New Jersey to only its second win in 13 games.

Sasha Vujacic added 15 points, Devin Harris had 14 points and eight assists, and Travis Outlaw had 13 points as the Nets had seven players in double figures.
