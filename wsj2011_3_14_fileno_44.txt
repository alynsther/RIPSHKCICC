Financial services company Reliance Capital Ltd. on Monday said it has agreed to sell a 26% stake in its life insurance unit to Japan-based Nippon Life Insurance Company Ltd for 30.62 billion rupees ($680 million).

The transaction pegs Reliance Life Insurance Company Ltd's total valuation at 115 billion rupees ($2.6 billion), the company said in a release.

The deal is subject to necessary regulatory approvals, it said.
