NEW DELHI -- Karur Vysya Bank,a regional bank, has appointed K. Venkataraman as managing director and chief executive officer effective June 1, according to a bank statement.

Mr. Venkataraman, currently, holds the same positions at SBI Global Factors, a financial services company of India's largest bank, State Bank of India.

Karur Vysya's current CEO and managing director P.T. Kuppuswamy's term ends on May 31, the bank said.
