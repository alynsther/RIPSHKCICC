Mumbai --India's Power Finance Corp. has raised 9.5 billion rupees by selling short-term debt, said two people familiar with the matter. 

The state-run lender to power projects privately placed commercial paper maturing in three months' time and paying a yield of 9.63%, the people, who declined to be named, told Dow Jones Newswires. 

They didn't specify what the money would be used for.
