Mumbai -- India's Reliance Capital Ltd has raised one billion rupees through sale of bonds, a person familiar with the matter said Tuesday. 

The private lender sold three-year bonds at a yield of 10.35% at par, the person, who declined to be named, told Dow Jones Newswires. 

The person didn't say what the money would be used for. A company spokesman didn't immediately respond to an emailed query.
