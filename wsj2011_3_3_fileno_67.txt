Ilya Kovalchuk scored his fifth winning goal in the last 11 games and the New Jersey Devils continued their amazing playoff push with a victory over the Tampa Bay Lightning on Wednesday night.

Martin Brodeur had 15 saves and Henrik Tallinder also scored as the Devils won for the 18th time in 22 games (18-2-2) and moved past Florida into 12th place in the Eastern Conference, nine points behind eighth-place Carolina.

Dominic Moore scored for Tampa Bay, which had a three-game winning streak snapped.

Dwayne Roloson made 25 saves, but he didn't have a chance on either Devils' goal.
