Three Rangers scored in a shootout to lead the Blueshirts to a win over the Washington Capitals on Monday night. 

Wojtek Wolski, Mats Zuccarello and Artem Anisimov all beat rookie Caps goaltender Braden Holtby in the shootout to end the game. 

The Capitals led 1-0 on the back of a Matt Hendricks second-period goal until Marian Gaborik knotted the score at 13:19 of the third period, notching his 16th goal of the season. 

Martin Biron stopped 22 of 23 shots during regular play and 2 of 4 in the shootout for the win.
