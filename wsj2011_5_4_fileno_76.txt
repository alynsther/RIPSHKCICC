Chinese social-networking company Renren Inc. said in its original prospectus filed April 15 that the number of active users of its website reached 31 million during the first quarter, up about 29% from 24 million in the fourth quarter. A Digital Media article on April 29 incorrectly said that users increased by 32% in that period.

Thomas Kosten researches addiction vaccines at Baylor College of Medicine in Houston. A Personal Journal article on Tuesday about such vaccines incorrectly said that he worked at Baylor University in Houston. 

Hong Kong's International Commerce Center uses innovative new software to guide its double-decker elevator system but is not the first tower in Hong Kong to use such elevators, as was stated in the April 12 Leisure & Arts article "Skyscraper, Moneymaker."
