Alex Ovechkin assisted on goals by Jason Chimera and Nicklas Backstrom, and rookie Braden Holtby made 24 saves in his return from the minors to lift the Washington Capitals to a victory over the New York Islanders on Thursday night in Uniondale.

Winger Michael Grabner scored the Islanders' only goal on an assist from Josh Bailey in the second period, while goalie Rick DiPietro made 22 saves.

Mr. Ovechkin had the primary assist on Mr. Chimera's first-period goal and Mr. Backstrom's tally in the second that put Washington ahead 2-0.
