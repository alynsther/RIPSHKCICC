Mumbai -- Ovira Logistics Pvt. Ltd has bought a 7.68% stake in Pipavav Shipyard Ltd from three IL&FS Group companies through open market deals, according to a filing with the Bombay Stock Exchange.

Ovira purchased 35.64 million shares at 80 rupees each from Infrastructure Leasing and Financial Services Ltd, 11.50 million shares at 83 rupees apiece from IL&FS Financial Services Ltd. and another 3.96 million at 82 rupees each from IL&FS Employee Welfare Trust, the data showed. 

Shares Of Pipavav Shipyard closed at 79.05 rupees, down 0.3% on the Bombay Stock Exchange.
