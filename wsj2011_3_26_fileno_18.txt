Dwight Howard had 21 points and 14 rebounds, and Hedo Turkoglu added 20 points and 13 assists to help the Orlando Magic hold off the Nets on Friday night.

The Magic led by 10 at the half before giving up the lead early in the opening minutes of the fourth quarter. But they rallied and used a 13-0 run to put the game away.

Anthony Morrow had 19 points for the Nets, while Jordan Farmar added 15 points and 16 assists. New Jersey lost for the fifth time in six games.
