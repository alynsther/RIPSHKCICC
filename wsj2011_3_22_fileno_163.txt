 PLC--Tuesday said it has acquired the assets of Bellary Steel Ltd for 2.20 billion rupees in cash. 

Bellary Steel had been taken over by its creditors, led by IFCI Ltd, which then sold off the assets to Sesa Goa. 

Sesa Goa said it will consider ways to restart construction at Bellary Steel's stee plant with a capacity to produce 500,000 metric tons a year. Apart from structure and machinery, Bellary also owns more than 700 acres of land.
