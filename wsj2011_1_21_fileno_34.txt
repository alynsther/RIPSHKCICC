Cam Ward made 39 saves and Chad LaRose, Jussi Jokinen, Brandon Sutter and Jeff Skinner scored in the Carolina Hurricanes' victory over the New York Rangers on Thursday night in Raleigh.

Tuomo Ruutu added two assists to help the Hurricanes snap a two-game losing streak.

Brandon Prust scored for the Rangers, spoiling Mr. Ward's shutout with 8:20 remaining in the game. 

Henrik Lundqvist made 35 saves for the injury-ravaged Rangers, who were coming off a 7-0 home victory over Toronto on Wednesday night.
