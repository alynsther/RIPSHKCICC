The average yield on six-month "jumbo" CDs, which typically require deposits of $95,000 or more, remained unchanged at 0.32% in the week ended Tuesday from the week earlier, according to Bankrate.com. The yield on five-year jumbos also remained unchanged, at 1.73%.

The average yields on small-denomination "savings" CDs also were mostly unchanged. The average six-month CD yield was unchanged at 0.29%, said Bankrate.com. The average two-year yield was unchanged at 0.71%. And the average five-year yield was unchanged at 1.70%.
