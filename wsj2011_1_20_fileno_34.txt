NEW DELHI -- TVS Motor Co. said Thursday third-quarter net profit more than doubled thanks to higher sales of motorcycles, scooters and three-wheeled vehicles.

Profit for the three months ended Dec. 31 climbed to 557.5 million rupees from 235.3 million rupees a year earlier, India's third-largest two-wheeler maker by sales said.

Sales surged 50% to 16.13 billion rupees from 10.73 billion rupees.

TVS sold 518,000 two-wheeled vehicles during the quarter, a 39% rise from a year earlier. The figure included 211,000 motorcycles, an increase of 40%.
