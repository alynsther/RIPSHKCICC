The Australian Open is known for its scorching days, but so far, temperatures have hovered in the mid-60s. Tournament meteorologist Bob Leighton says the Open's Extreme Heat Policy is unlikely to be triggered. Serbia's Novak Djokovic, who has battled heat exhaustion, isn't complaining. "I'm probably one of the few players that love rain," he said.

Kikkan Randall is seeking to become the first U.S. woman to win an overall World Cup cross-country title. Ms. Randall, who trained with Norwegian team members in the offseason, has growing confidence. "Now, when I start a World Cup race, I know I can win," Ms. Randall said after winning a race this past weekend in Liberec, Czech Republic.
