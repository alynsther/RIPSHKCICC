SYDNEY -- Leighton Holdings Ltd. has completed the sale of a 35% stake in its Leighton India subsidiary to local conglomerate Welspun Group, the company said. 

The sale, announced in December, was reported at the time to be worth A$104 million ($104 million). 

It's a strategic move that aims to "capitalize on the many concessions based projects and infrastructure opportunities that are emerging in India," said Chief Executive David Stewart in a statement.
