Q: A new year brings new opportunities. With many companies hiring once again, you're due for a job interview. But it's been so long. What is a man to wear?

A: The general rule remains: Wear a dark suit—navy, gray, charcoal or black—plain white or blue shirt, conservative tie and smart, clean shoes. Sounds obvious, but you'd be surprised how many people get this wrong, usually for one of three reasons: a) They're young and inexperienced; b) They've been stuck at an organization so long that they've been getting away with being schlubby; or c) They're simply oblivious.
