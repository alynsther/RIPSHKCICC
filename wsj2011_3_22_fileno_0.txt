Bangalore --Indian consumer goods maker Jyothy Laboratories Ltd announced that its laundry unit has acquired laundry services provider Diamond Fabcare Pvt. Ltd. for an undisclosed amount. 

Jyothy Fabricare Services Ltd's acquisition of a 100% stake in Delhi-based Diamond Fabcare will enhance the unit's presence in the north Indian market, the company said in a statement. 

Diamond Fabcare provides dry cleaning, laundry, starching, ironing and other services, the statement said. 

Jyothy Laboratories, which sells laundry products under the popular Ujala brand, entered retail laundry services under the Fabric Spa brand in the southern city of Bangalore in 2009. The business is being handled by its Jyothy Fabricare unit.
