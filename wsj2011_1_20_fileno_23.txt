NEW DELHI -- India's federal government currently has no plan to raise diesel prices, the country's new oil minister S.  Jaipal Reddy said Thursday, adding that gasoline prices will continue to be market-driven.

"There is no proposal on the anvil for a diesel price hike," Mr. Reddy, who replaced  Murli Deora as the oil minister, told reporters.
