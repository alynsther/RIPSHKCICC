LONDON—High-speed rail operator Eurostar says that revenue rose by 12% in 2010 as the business travel market rebounded.

Eurostar said Friday that revenue rose to £760 million ($1.2 billion) compared to £675.5 million in 2009.

The total number of passengers rose by 3% to 9.5 million. Eurostar operates services linking London and Paris, and London and Brussels.
