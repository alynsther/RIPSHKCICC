Clayton Stoner scored and Jose Theodore made 21 saves as the Minnesota Wild beat the New Jersey Devils on Tuesday night.

Ilya Kovalchuk scored for the Devils, who have only two wins in 15 games.

Mr. Stoner scored his first NHL goal 1:14 into the third period to snap a 1-1 tie.

Cal Clutterbuck also scored for Wild, who won for the third time in four games. It was Minnesota's first win in New Jersey after four losses and a tie.
