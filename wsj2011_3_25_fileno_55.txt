The decision by the NFL's owners to move kickoffs to the 35-yard line from the 30 will not have a major impact on the Jets' use of their versatile offensive player Brad Smith, general manager Mike Tannenbaum recently told ESPN 1050. "Kickoffs are obviously a factor, but every time he touches the ball, good things happen," Tannenbaum said.

—Mike Sielski
