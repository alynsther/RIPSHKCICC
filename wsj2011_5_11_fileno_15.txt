NEW DELHI --India's federal cabinet has approved a foreign investment of $200 million into GMR Airport Holdings, a unit of GMR Infrastructure Ltd.

The investment was made by Standard Chartered Private Equity (Mauritius) Ltd., JM Financial-Old Lane India Corporate Opportunities Fund Ltd. Mauritius and NYLIM Jacob Ballas India Fund, Mauritius, which subscribed to the compulsorily convertible preference share issue of GMR Airport Holdings.
