 A congressional report found there were 14 known or possible carcinogens present in drilling fluids used in hydraulic fracturing, a process used to recover natural gas. A U.S. News article on Wednesday incorrectly stated there were 29. 

 U.S. Rep. Anna Eshoo in California has criticized plans for an elevated rail structure for high-speed rail in Silicon Valley, but supports upgrading existing commuter rail tracks so high-speed trains can run on them. An article in some editions Thursday gave an incomplete description of her view. 

 Hope Reichbach, communications  director for City Council Member Stephen Levin, was found dead inside her apartment in Brooklyn Thursday. Her title was incorrectly given as chief of staff in an article Friday.
