The SLS Hotel at Beverly Hills is located in Los Angeles. A Wednesday Property Report article and photo caption incorrectly said the hotel is in Beverly Hills.

As of Jan. 4, the WikiLeaks website says the organization has released 1,999 of the 251,287 confidential State Department cables it claims to have. A Dec. 18 This Week column and a Dec. 27 World News article incorrectly said that Wikileaks had released thousands of State Department documents.

The Toronto Blue Jays finished second in the American League Eastern Division in 2006. A Sports article Friday incorrectly said Toronto never finished better than third in the division during J.P. Ricciardi's eight years as their general manager.
