MUMBAI --Templeton Strategic Emerging Markets Fund has invested $17.8 million in Apar Industries.

The emerging market fund, part of the Templeton Asset Management group, said the investment will help finance Apar's ongoing capacity expansion plan and strengthen its working capital base. 

The investment in Apar, which makes and exports transformer oils and conductors, will also fit in with the fund manager's global emphasis on the infrastructure sector, Templeton said in a press release. 

It didn't give further details.
