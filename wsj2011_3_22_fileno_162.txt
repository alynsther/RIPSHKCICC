MUMBAI -- India's Tata Sons Ltd has raised 3.85 billion rupees by selling bonds, a person familiar with the matter said Monday. 

The holding company for the diversified Tata group privately placed five-year bonds at a yield of 9.90% at par, the person, who declined to be named, told Dow Jones Newswires. 

The person said the original issue size was 1.25 billion rupees, but the company had an option to raise an unspecified, additional amount, which it exercised. Details on the company's plans for the funds weren't immediately known. 

The company couldn't be reached for comment.
