Joni Pitkanen scored at 3:58 of overtime to give the Carolina Hurricanes a win over the Islanders on Friday night in Raleigh, N.C. Erik Cole set up the extra period for the Hurricanes with a tying goal at 15:23 of the third. Hurricane Jeff Skinner scored his 25th goal and had an assist on Pitkanen's winner. 

Michael Grabner and Frans Nielsen each had a goal and an assist for the Islanders.
