E'Twaun Moore scored 19 points and JuJuan Johnson added 16 with 16 rebounds Friday night as third-seeded Purdue routed the St. Peter's Peacocks of Jersey City in the second round of the NCAA's Southwest Region in Chicago. Saint Peter's shot a paltry 26% in the first half, and Jeron Belin led them in scoring with 12. The 14th-seeded Peacocks were the MAAC Tournament champions this year.
