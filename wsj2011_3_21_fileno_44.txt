MUMBAI --India's Voltas Ltd. and Germany's Kion Group GmbH have agreed to form a joint venture for developing, manufacturing and servicing forklift trucks and warehousing equipment in India. 

The joint venture, to be formed with Kion Group unit Linde Material Handling GmbH, is expected to start operations in April, the companies said in a joint statement. 

Kion Group will hold a majority stake in the joint venture, it added.
