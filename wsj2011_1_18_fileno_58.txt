Monta Ellis had 26 points and nine assists to help the Golden State Warriors beat the Nets for the sixth straight time on Monday.

David Lee added 24 points and 10 rebounds for the Warriors, who have won two of their first three games in a key stretch to the season that features 13 of 14 games at home.

Brook Lopez scored 20 points and Devin Harris added 19 for New Jersey, which has lost six straight and 11 of 12 games.

The latest loss completed an 0-4 road trip for the Nets, who stayed close but never were able to seriously threaten the Warriors.
