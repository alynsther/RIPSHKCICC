Anthony Morrow scored 10 points during a key second-half run in his first game in a month to lead the Nets to victory over the Detroit Pistons Friday night in Newark. The victory was the Nets' 12th, tying their total for all of last season. Brook Lopez had 15 points, Devin Harris added 12 and nine assists, and Kris Humphries had 11 points and 12 rebounds as the energized Nets won their second straight game.
