MUMBAI )--Simbhaoli Sugars Ltd said its board has approved a business restructuring plan under which it will spin off its alcohol and power businesses to subsidiary companies. 

Simbhaoli will transfer its potable alcohol business, currently under its Simbhaoli distillery division, to a unit, the company said. 

It will transfer its power business--under its co-generation division--to a unit to enable power generation capacity expansion, it also said
