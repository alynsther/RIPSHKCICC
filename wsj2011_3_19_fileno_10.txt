Tyler Zeller scored 32 points, John Henson added 28, as North Carolina used its size to overcome sloppiness in an NCAA tournament victory over scrappy Long Island University on Friday night in Charlotte, N.C. The second-seeded Tar Heels (27-7) just wore down the 15th-seeded Blackbirds (27-6). Julian Boyd scored 18 points, C.J. Garner added 16 and Jamal Olasewere had 15 for LIU.
